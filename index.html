Conway's Game of Life

Conway's Game of Life is a cellular automata. It shows how complex behavior can arise from simple rules. The state of each cell is determined by the state of it's 8 surrounding cells. Conways game of life is traditionally set on an infinite grid. I have made a small change and this grid wraps around. What goes off the right side of the screen appears on the left, same with up and down.
